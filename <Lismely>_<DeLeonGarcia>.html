Tips for Choosing a Plan

  1. List your healthcare must-haves and wants.
  2. Compare coverage options and benefits.
  3. Check if your preferred doctors are in-network.
  4. Check if your medications are covered.
  5. Read the policy details and disclosure carefully.
  6. Write down your agent's name and contacts for later questions.
